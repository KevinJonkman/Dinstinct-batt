## Key Observations

1. **Significantly higher capacity than SGS** - 99.3 Wh vs 80.7 Wh
2. **Voltage plateau** around 3.5-3.7V (most stable region)
3. **Low temperature rise** - only +4°C above ambient at 12A
4. **CV phase at end** - current tapered from 12A → 1.7A at 2.69V

## Issues Found During Test

1. **SCPI truncation bug** - Current showed -11A instead of -12A (fixed in v7.4)
2. **Discharge cutoff bug** - Required both V AND I conditions (fixed in v7.4)
3. **Voltage display** - ESP32 showed 2.69V when Delta LCD showed 2.70V
